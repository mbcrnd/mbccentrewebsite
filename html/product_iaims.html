Integrated Accounting Information Management System (IAIMS)


MBC Software Integrated Accounting System is an integrated system that integrates Inventory, Sale, Accounts Receivable, Purchase, Accounts Payable and General Ledger. Using IAIMS, you can quickly and easily retrieve everything from your inventory to your business's profit and loss statement and financial statements.
